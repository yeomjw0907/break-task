# TaskBrick MVP

## Product

TaskBrick is a desktop-first productivity layer for knowledge workers. It does not replace Notion or calendar tools. It turns task completion into a short arcade-like reward loop with visible daily score, combo, coins, and a quick end-of-day recap.

## Target users

- Desk workers who already manage tasks in Notion or calendar tools
- Founders and operators who want a stronger sense of progress during the day
- Teams that care about visible daily momentum more than deep project planning

## Design system direction

- Core tone: professional productivity tool first, arcade reward layer second
- Everyday UI: calm, structured, low-noise, high readability
- Reward moments: sharp, fast, bright, and clearly separated from the base UI
- Typography: expressive sans for interface, mono for points and scoreboards
- Color model: warm neutral surfaces with a focused amber accent
- Motion: minimal at rest, punchy on completion

## Market view

The category exists, but most products lean into habit-tracking, self-care, or fantasy RPG aesthetics. The open space is a work-oriented system that stays credible for professionals while making completion feel satisfying.

Reference products reviewed:

- Habitica
- Finch
- LifeUp
- MainQuest

## MVP scope

- Daily task board
- Priority-based scoring engine
- Combo and coin rewards
- Completion feed
- Daily summary
- High-score persistence
- Previous run snapshots

## V1 after MVP
